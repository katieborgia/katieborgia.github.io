533 Digital Fabrication


533 Digital Fabrication is a Master's level class at The University of Washington offered by the department of Human Centered Design and Engineering.

In this class, students learn how to use, design, and model both 2D and 3D designs using the following CAD software:
- Rhino
- Grasshopper
- Cura Ultimaker
- Adobe Illustrator
- Adobe Photoshop

Fabrication techniques covered this quarter include:
- CAD modeling
- 3D printing
- molding
- casting
- rendering
- slicing

As part of the course, I used this Github site to document my progress through all the assignments. Please enjoy my work.
